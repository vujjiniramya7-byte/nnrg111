--- ABOUT NNRG ---
Nalla Narasimha Reddy Education Society (NNRG) is a premier group of institutions established in 2009 in Hyderabad, Telangana. Founded to provide world-class technical education. Integrated Contiguous Campus with three schools: School of Engineering, School of Pharmacy, and School of Management Sciences. Affiliated to JNTUH, approved by AICTE (New Delhi) and PCI (New Delhi).

Accreditations: NAAC A+ Grade, NBA (CSE, ECE, ME - 2018), UGC Section 2(f), ISO 9001:2015, NPTEL Local Chapter Code 421.
Vision: To be a premier institution ensuring globally competent and ethically strong professionals.
Salient Features: State-of-the-Art Laboratories, Advanced English Communication Labs, 600 Mbps Internet bandwidth with Wi-Fi, Central Library with 30000+ volumes, Digital Library with NPTEL video resources, R&D Cell, Placement Training from First Year, IIIC, EDC.
